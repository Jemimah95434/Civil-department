Overview of Civil Engineering

The Division of Civil Engineering has been in existence since the inception of Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences in 1986 and has grown into a full-fledged Division with specializations in Structural Engineering, Environment and Water Resources Engineering. The B.Tech Civil Engineering program is accredited by NBA. It has grown steadily with its parent institution and has turned out 31 batches of Civil Engineering Graduates. Many of our alumni hold prestigious positions in leading industries, government and academic institutions world-wide. The Division has well qualified faculty members with different areas of specialization with good academic, field and research background.

Our Vision

To be an eminent Division producing competent civil engineering graduates, developing perceptible research to render quality professional and community service.

Our Mission

To create an ideal ambience for learning by integrating theory with practice. To provide students with opportunities to pursue research to find solutions to the challenges in civil engineering. To interact with industries in addressing issues concerning infrastructure, resources utilization and environmental protection.

Career Prospects

Civil engineering is one of the most versatile branches of engineering. Civil engineers design, construct, supervise, operate, and maintain construction projects such as roads, buildings, tunnels, airports, dams, and sewage systems.

Placement Opportunities

Government Sector: UPSC - IES, CPWD, CWC, Railways, Military. PSC - PWD, Jal Nigam, PCL. Public Sector: NTPC, NHPC, RITES, BHEL, ONGC, IOCL, etc. Private Sector: Real estate, Dams, Planning & Management, Airports, and more.
